1990-2010

August 26, 2010 is the 30th anniversary of the establishment of shenzhen special economic zone. In the past 30 years, shenzhen has developed from a fishing village to a modern metropolis, creating a world-renowned "shenzhen speed". CDP has grown at an annual rate of 27%, three times the national average. There is no other city like this in the world.
